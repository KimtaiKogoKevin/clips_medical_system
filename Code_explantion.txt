		3. ACTUAL TESTS
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
		1. Skin scratch test - the medic exposes the skin to certain allergens and observes the reactions 
		2. Total Ige blood test - the medic measures the haemoglobin protein level in the patients blood
		

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
		4. DIagnosis
-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
		1. Positive reaction to allergens , avoid the allergens that caused the reaction and recommend lifestyle changes and medication 
		2. Positive allergic reaction shown by increased haemoglobin in blood when exposed to allergens , prescribe lifestyle changes and medication
		3. No reaction to both tests indicates the obswerevd symptoms may be of another disease , explore further testing
